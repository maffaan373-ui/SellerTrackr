Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a sale?
Go to Reports page from the sidebar. Click the Add Sale button, fill in product name, amount, storefront and other details, then click Save.
What happens when I reach the 50 sale limit?
On the Free plan, you can track up to 50 sales per month. When you hit the limit, new sales won't be saved until next month or until you upgrade to Premium.
How do I upgrade to Premium?
Click Pricing in the sidebar or the Upgrade button at the top. Select Premium (₨900/month), send payment via Easypaisa, JazzCash or Bank Transfer, then submit your Transaction ID. Account will be upgraded within 2–12 hours after admin verification.
Can I cancel my Premium subscription?
Yes, anytime. Premium access stays active until the end of the current billing period. Email support@sellertrackr.com to cancel.
How do I change my password?
Go to Settings in the sidebar, scroll to Change Password, enter your new password (min 8 characters), confirm it, then click Update Password.
