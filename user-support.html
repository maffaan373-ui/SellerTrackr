Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add a sale to my account?
Go to the Reports page and click the "Add Sale" button. Fill in the product name, amount, storefront, and channel, then click Save.
What happens when I hit the 50 sale limit?
You'll be notified when approaching the limit. Existing data is still viewable, but new sales won't save until you upgrade to Premium or the next billing month starts.
How do I upgrade to Premium?
Go to the Pricing page and click "Upgrade to Premium". You'll be taken to the payment page — we accept Easypaisa, JazzCash, and bank transfer.
Can I cancel my Premium subscription?
Yes. Email support@sellertrackr.com and we'll cancel immediately. You keep Premium access until end of your current billing period.
How do I change my password?
Go to Settings, scroll to the "Change Password" section, enter your new password, confirm it, and click Update Password.
