Values

What we believe in

01
Honesty first
Nomino never pretends to be human, never hides its limitations, and always gives you accurate information or tells you when it doesn't know.
02
Language equity
AI must work equally well in Urdu, Arabic, and Hindi as it does in English. We invest heavily in multilingual quality — not as an afterthought.
03
Privacy by design
Your conversations belong to you. We don't use your data to train models without explicit consent. You can delete everything, anytime.
04
Accessible pricing
The best AI tools should not cost a fortune. We keep prices honest and offer a meaningful free tier so anyone can experience Nomino.
