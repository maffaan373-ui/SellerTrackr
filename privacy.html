1. Information We Collect

SellerTrackr collects information you provide directly when you create an account or use our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you sign up.
  • Sales Data: Product names, amounts, store names, channels, and sale dates that you enter.
  • Payment Information: Payment records for Premium plan subscriptions (processed securely; we do not store card details).
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and device/browser information to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the SellerTrackr platform
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Process Premium plan payments and send receipts
  • Send important service updates and security alerts
  • Generate analytics and reports based on your sales data
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data to third parties. We may share information only with:

  • Supabase: Our database provider that stores your data securely.
  • Payment Processors: For processing Premium subscriptions (JazzCash, EasyPaisa).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) ensures you can only access your own data
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

5. Cookies

SellerTrackr uses essential cookies only to maintain your login session and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect app functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information in your profile settings.
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Export your sales data as CSV (Premium feature).

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@sellertrackr.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained for service improvement.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@sellertrackr.com or visit our Contact page.
