Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add my first sale?
Go to Reports page and click "Add Sale". Fill in product name, amount, store, channel, and status. Your sale will be saved instantly and reflected in your dashboard analytics.
What is the free plan limit?
The free plan allows up to 50 sales per month. Once you reach this limit, you'll need to upgrade to Premium (₨900/month) to continue adding sales. Premium gives you unlimited sales and all advanced features.
How do I upgrade to Premium?
Click "Upgrade" in your dashboard sidebar or visit the Pricing page. We accept payments via JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and bank transfer. Your account is upgraded instantly after payment confirmation.
Can I export my sales data?
Yes — CSV export is available for Premium users. Go to Reports and click the "Export CSV" button. Free users can upgrade to access this feature.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All data is stored securely using Supabase with row-level security. Your sales data is only accessible to you. We use HTTPS encryption for all connections and never share your data with third parties.
How do I reset my password?
On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it in your inbox.
Which platforms are supported?
SellerTrackr supports Daraz, Instagram, WhatsApp, and any custom storefront. You can track sales from multiple platforms in one dashboard and filter by store or channel.

Still need help?

Our support team is ready to assist you. We typically respond within 24 hours.
