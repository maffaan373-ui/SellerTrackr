Frequently Asked Questions
How do I upgrade to Pro?
Click Upgrade Now on any paid plan and complete the payment via Easypaisa, JazzCash or SadaPay. Your account upgrades immediately after confirmation.
What is the difference between Pro and Business?
Pro covers all core features for individual sellers. Business adds multiple storefronts, team access, WhatsApp alerts, and GST calculator for growing teams.
What happens to my data if I downgrade?
Your sales data stays completely safe. You will lose access to premium features but all records remain intact.
Can I switch between Monthly and Annual?
Yes. You can switch plans anytime. Annual billing saves you 25% compared to paying monthly.
